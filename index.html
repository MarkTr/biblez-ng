BibleZ

read and study the bible!

Demo FirefoxOS Firefox GitHub

BibleZ is an easy to use offline bible reader app for Firefox and your FirefoxOS device! Enjoy the full bible at your fingertips.

Features

  • many available bibles in different languages (e.g. ESV, KJV, NET, ...)
  • notes
  • bookmarks
  • highlights
  • history of last read scriptures
  • no internet connection required (only for downloading modules)

BibleZ is based on the SWORD Project (http://crosswire.org)
